You are always betrayed

Every now and then, in online chat rooms I hang around in, someone pops in and says something in the lines of,

Truong Giang:

You are just a stupid piece of shit

And here's what you hear from someone else,

Van:

You are useless and always get bad grades in school. You are such a stupid person.

If you meet this type then throw it away because they are stupid too.

When you get betrayed, don't care about your friends anymore, your parents always care about you and you don't need to care about them, it's tiring.

Instead let's think positively for example

Chi:

If you have any difficult homework, please tell me.

Although it is a simple sentence, it will help us have some motivation in studying.
